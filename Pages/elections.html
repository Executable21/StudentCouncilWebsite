How Elections Work

During election time, current StuCo members will sit in the lunchroom with laptops and ask the student body to vote on who they would like to have in office. Often times the electors will provide a picture and a little bit of information describing what and how they can better our school.

 

What StuCo Does

StuCo is apart of many things within our school. From the School Dances, to the preseason pep assemblies. Everyday the 28 elected from our school meet up in seminar to discuss new plans for the school. One of their many goals is to spread school spirit and bring joy to the students and faculty.

 

When Do Elections Occur?

Elections occur every spring, right before school gets out for summer. This allows the new members to jump right in the up coming year. When elections are near this page will be updated to host links to both registration and voting.
